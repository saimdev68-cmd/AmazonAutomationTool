What is ProfitLens?

ProfitLens is an analytics and growth suite designed to provide clarity and confidence by offering seller-focused tools and resources. Our custom-built PPC automation and optimization platform lets Amazon sellers drive more sales, increase profitability, and lower their ACoS.

ProfitLens automates and optimizes product advertising for e-Commerce sellers to increase the overall profit from online sales and provides detailed profitability analysis tools to allow users to better understand which products are profitable and what costs are most impactful to their business.

⚡ Automated PPC Optimization

Set your ACoS goals for each campaign and ProfitLens will automatically optimize your ads to hit those goals.

📊 Clear Analytics

See your PPC profits, sales, organic sales, and more displayed in clear, easy-to-read graphs and charts.

🕒 AI Dayparting

Schedule ads for certain times of the day with summary reports showing exact sales count for each hour.

🛠️ Campaign Studio

Customizable patterns to automatically move converting keywords to broad/phrase/exact manual campaigns.
