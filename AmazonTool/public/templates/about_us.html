About ProfitLens

ProfitLens was built to solve one of the most complex problems in ecommerce: how to scale Amazon advertising profitably.

Founded in 2017, ProfitLens began as a data-first platform designed to give Amazon sellers true visibility into performance and profitability. As brands grew and advertising became more competitive, it became clear that insight alone wasn't enough — sellers needed automation, intelligence, and control at scale.

Today, ProfitLens is a powerful Amazon advertising platform that combines advanced automation, machine learning, and human expertise to help brands and agencies maximize return on ad spend while maintaining full transparency and strategic oversight.

Built for Real-World Amazon Complexity

Amazon advertising isn't static. It changes daily — bids, placements, search behavior, competition, and margins are always in motion. ProfitLens was designed specifically for this environment.

Our platform continuously evaluates performance signals across campaigns, keywords, and products, then takes action to optimize toward your goals — whether that's ACoS, TACoS, profitability, or growth. Unlike "black box" tools, ProfitLens gives teams visibility into why changes are made and the flexibility to tailor strategies by brand, category, or marketplace.
